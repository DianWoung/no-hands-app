# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## Orders & Shipping

**Q: How can I check my order status?**
A: You can check your order status by providing our AI assistant with your order number. For example, say "What is the status of order #12345?".

**Q: What is the return policy?**
A: We offer a 30-day return policy on most items. The product must be in its original packaging and in new condition. Please note that some items, like software, are non-returnable.

**Q: How long does shipping take?**
A: Standard shipping usually takes 3-5 business days. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout for an additional fee.

## Product & Warranty

**Q: What is the warranty on electronics?**
A: All new electronic products come with a one-year limited manufacturer's warranty that covers defects in materials and workmanship. You can purchase extended warranty coverage (like AppleCare+) for additional protection.

**Q: My device won't turn on, what should I do?**
A: First, please ensure your device is charged for at least 30 minutes using the provided charging cable. Then, try a force restart. For an iPhone, quickly press and release the volume up button, then the volume down button, and then press and hold the side button until you see the Apple logo.

**Q: Are the products new or refurbished?**
A: We sell only brand-new, factory-sealed products unless explicitly marked as "Refurbished" in the product title.
